How to Protect Yourself

Tell a Trusted Adult

If you feel unsafe or someone is bullying or harassing you, always talk to a parent, teacher, or another trusted adult who can help you.

Keep Evidence

If the bullying or harassment happens online or through messages, keep screenshots or records. These can be very useful when reporting the issue.

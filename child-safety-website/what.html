What are Bullying, Harassment, and Violence?

Bullying

Bullying is repeated aggressive behavior intended to hurt, intimidate, or humiliate someone. It can include physical aggression, verbal insults, spreading rumors, exclusion, or even cyberbullying.

Harassment

Harassment is unwanted behavior that demeans or offends someone. It is often related to personal traits like race, religion, or disability, and includes offensive jokes, comments, or stalking.

Violence

Violence is the use of force or threats to cause harm to others, property, or oneself. It can involve fighting, abuse, assault, or threats, often leading to serious physical and emotional damage.
